Never Trust Your Chef was created after three close friends from Harsh, Aniket and Harshu, Ghaziabad, decided to bring the authentic Indian taste to the rest of the world.

The authentic taste comes from family recipes and from fresh, simple and tasteful ingredients straight from home. In every cuisine from Never Trust Your Chef there is a bit of true Mexican culture and flavor along with chef's emotions.

As the name suggest, trusting chef here might not be the best thing you thought of leaving our home, but it is out of the best possibilites which will make your day. Whether you are coming back home after a long tiring work or planning to just have a great time with your family and friends, we got everything covered.

We currently have seven restaurent nation wide and more are coming soon.
